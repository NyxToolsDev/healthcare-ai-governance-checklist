# Healthcare AI Governance Readiness Checklist

A 40-item self-assessment to find out whether your healthcare organization is ready to govern AI tools under HIPAA, the Colorado AI Act, and emerging federal requirements.

## What This Is

A structured checklist across 7 areas that matter for healthcare AI governance:

1. **Policy and Leadership** — Do you have a written AI policy? Does someone own it?
2. **HIPAA and PHI Protections** — Are staff trained on what not to paste into AI tools? Are your BAAs updated?
3. **Vendor Evaluation** — Are you vetting AI vendors or trusting the sales deck?
4. **Training and Awareness** — Is training tied to AI tool access, or is it a checkbox exercise?
5. **Shadow AI and Monitoring** — Are staff using tools you don't know about? (Probably.)
6. **Incident Response** — What's the plan when someone puts patient data into the wrong tool?
7. **Regulatory Awareness** — Colorado AI Act, HIPAA Security Rule update, EU AI Act, ~200 state bills

Score yourself Yes / Partial / No on each item. Takes about 20 minutes. You'll know where the gaps are.

## Who This Is For

- CIOs, CISOs, and IT Directors at healthcare organizations
- Compliance Officers assessing AI readiness
- Privacy Officers updating HIPAA programs for AI
- Anyone responsible for AI governance at a healthcare org

## How to Use It

1. Open [`AI-Governance-Readiness-Checklist.md`](AI-Governance-Readiness-Checklist.md)
2. Work through each section with the people who'd know the answers
3. Tally your scores using the summary table at the end
4. Prioritize sections with the most "No" answers

## Why This Exists

58% of healthcare organizations using AI tools have no formal governance policy. Three major regulatory frameworks are converging in 2025-2026. This checklist won't solve governance for you, but it'll show you exactly where you need to focus.
